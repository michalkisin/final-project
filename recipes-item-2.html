Birthday Cake Shots

Bright pink shots that taste just like birthday cake!

Ingredients

    Produce
  • 1/4 cup Betty Crocker™ ready-to-spread vanilla frosting
  • 1 teaspoon Betty Crocker™ pink gel food color, divided
  • 1/4 cup rainbow sprinkles
  • 1 cup cake vodka, chilled
  • 1 cup Baileys™ Irish Cream or Godiva™ White Chocolate Liqueur, chilled
    • Steps
  • Tint Betty Crockery™ frosting with 1/2 teaspoon gel food coloring. Spread on a small plate and dip the rims of 6 shot glasses in the frosting.
  • Then dip the shot glasses in the sprinkles. Set aside.
  • Pour in prepared shot glasses and serve immediately.
