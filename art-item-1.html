Bubble Bath Mermaid Art

When land-locked, the best place to be a mermaid is in the bath, right?! I recently launched a new item I've been working on, these Mermaid Bath Bombs, and with those fizzing in the tub, painting a mermaid in the bath came quickly to mind. After I painted one, I quickly had an idea for another, and then pretty soon I'de painted 4! My first painting was a purple-haired mermaid with a cat. She's in a classic tub. The bathtub I imagine everyone should have in their house (I don't have a tub or even a house!) but the imaginary house I live in has one in every bathroom.

Teacup Mermaid

Next up was a blondy bathing in a shell. She got an intricate shell and starfish crown looks so comfy in her shell. I recently found a giant clam shell like this and it inspired this mini-mermaid bathing vessel...

Bathing Shell Mermaid

The steps...

Last, but not least I did a mermaid in a barrel, with seaweed green hair. I mostly love that it feels like she is flicking her tail, just because I did 3 little water drops off the top. So much action from 3 little drops! I also love how her tail came out. I used the spotting technique with lots of paint over an all-wetted tail surface.
